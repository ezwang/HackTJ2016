Help/About

Many language-learning resources lack one major feature – effectively teaching pronunciation. WordPolo is made to help you learn how to both translate and pronounce phrases in Chinese. WordPolo is essentially a two-in-one flash card program. To practice a previously-made set of cards, simply start typing the topic of your choice in the 'Label Name' text box and click 'LOAD'. If you want to make your own set or want to edit a previously-made set, enter the Mandarin characters in the 'Characters' textbox and the English translation in the 'Translation' textbox, then press 'ADD WORD'. When you're done editing a set, you can click 'SAVE' to allow other users to use your set. For actual practicing, there are two options: 'Characters' and 'Translation'. 'Characters' will display the Chinese characters and you will then speak the characters. If your pronunciation is incorrect, you will be allowed to try again. 'Translation' is the same, except you will be given the English translation and then you will have to pronounce the phrase in Chinese.
